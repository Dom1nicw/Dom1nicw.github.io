About Me

Hi, I'm Dom. After graduating Exeter University (July 2022) I decided to go for a career in tech. Since graduating I completed the iO Academy coding bootcamp (March 2023 - June 2023) where I trained to be a full stack developer. I have found that I really enjoy the problem solving and non repeating nature of the subject.

I first started programming whilst studying maths at Exeter, where I used MATLAB and RStudio to make mathematical models and analyse stats and data. I found coding to be a very enjoyable part of my degree which is why I decided to persue a career in it. Whilst I was at iO I learnt how to write better quality and more maintainable code, as well as more advanced concepts such as object-oriented programming, unit testing and frameworks. I also enjoyed collaborating on projects at iO, using GitHub and practicing scrum and agile methodologies.

Outside of coding, my main hobbies include playing cricket, football and listening to drum and bass music.
